About the artist

Hello! I'm Roxan,


As a child, I drew on everything. I drew on books, dolls, walls, floors, the cabinet doors, and the dogs... But thankfully, when I first got a drawing tablet at age 12, I completely fell in love with digital art form and have stuck with it ever since then.


Prior to college, I was already very proficient in using Adobe Photoshop, Painter and Paint Tool SAI. This was especially helpful in getting into Seneca College's Animation Program, and from where I learned how to animate on paper, on Adobe Flash, and on Toonboom.


To explore new forms of digital media, I then chose Game Art and Design as a specialization which focused more on modeling, texturing, rigging and animating using 3DS Max, Zbrush, xNormal, nDO and Unreal Engine. Outside of school, I also taught myself how to model and animate using Autodesk Maya. I have an innate ability to not just quickly learn computer programs, but how to successfully combine my artistic skills with my computer skills.


The next step in my journey is to obtain a position at an exciting studio that will enable me to continue learning and growing as an artist and as an animator. I'm very eager to be a part of this industry that, similar to myself, keeps redefining itself and pushing the envelope.
